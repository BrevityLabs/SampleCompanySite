Corporate Social Responsibility

Supporting Solar enablement in rural parts of Karnataka

While the majority of India rural population go without the basic eminities like drinking water and electricity, government and non-government organizations (NGOs) are putting a huge effort to reach these to them. We have partnered with a low cost solar equipment manufacturer to install and implement solar panels in villages 'adopted' by us. Our employees draw a huge inspiration from activities towards a noble cause like this.
